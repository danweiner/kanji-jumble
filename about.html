About Kanji Jumble

This page is a way to help people learn Japanese characters

By breaking the characters down into components, it helps visual learners to see the smaller components of a more complex character

  1. First the components are displayed
  2. Then you try to draw the character on the canvas
  3. Then you click the reveal button to see if you got the kanji correct
